Every place we have explored

24 real places across the Emirates — the landscapes behind every design

This is the map behind the brand: every desert, dune field, wadi, mountain trail and beach we have explored across the UAE. Each place has its own guide with GPS coordinates, live weather, honest difficulty ratings and a packing list tailored to the terrain.

Dunes

🏜Big RedDubai · DunesThe most iconic dune in the UAE. Towering red sand dunes perfect for sandboarding and 4x4 adventures. Best visited at sunrise for dramatic golden light.🗼Falaj Al Mualla TowerUmm Al Quwain · DunesA lone historic watchtower standing in the open desert near Falaj Al Mualla — a striking, photogenic landmark and a favourite stop on an Umm Al Quwain desert drive.🏜Half DesertDubai · DunesGentle, rolling dunes close to the city — the perfect place to air down and learn desert driving before tackling the bigger stuff.🌅Liwa OasisAbu Dhabi · DunesHome to some of the largest sand dunes on earth. The Empty Quarter starts here. An otherworldly landscape of mega-dunes and silence.🏜Maleiha Desert DriveSharjah · DunesA scenic desert drive from Al Khatim toward Maleiha, passing the dramatic red dunes of Al Faya — a favourite 4x4 route for dune driving, photography and desert camping.🪨Mleiha DesertSharjah · DunesAn archaeological desert of golden dunes and wind-carved rock outcrops. Otherworldly formations, ancient tombs and Fossil Rock make it feel like another planet.

Camping

🌌Al Quaa DesertAbu Dhabi · CampingOne of the darkest skies in the Emirates. Deep in the southern desert, far from any city glow, it's a magnet for stargazers and astrophotographers chasing the Milky Way.🏝Al Rams BeachRas Al Khaimah · CampingA calm, mangrove-fringed beach on the Saraya Islands near Al Rams, RAK — great for kayaking, paddleboarding and relaxed beach camping with mountain views.🦌Crescent Moon LakeDubai · CampingA quiet desert lake where Arabian oryx, gazelle and deer come to drink at dawn and dusk. One of the best spots near the city for wildlife and birdlife.⛺Desert Camping (Lake View)Dubai · CampingA hidden, little-known camping spot tucked beside a desert lake — calm water, soft dunes and almost no crowds. A local secret for a peaceful overnight.🏕Hatta MountainsDubai · CampingDubai's hidden mountain retreat. Kayak the turquoise dam, mountain bike through trails, or camp under stars. A complete outdoor escape.💙Love LakeDubai · CampingTwo heart-shaped lakes carved into the Al Qudra desert, fringed with greenery and birdlife. A short, easy drive from the city straight into the wilderness.⛺Wadi KubRas Al Khaimah · CampingA remote wild-camping spot in the Hajar mountains near Masafi — rocky, quiet and far from the crowds, for self-sufficient campers and off-road adventurers.🦩Zakher LakeAbu Dhabi · CampingA reed-fringed desert lake near Al Ain, famous for flamingos and birdlife — a peaceful spot for fishing, photography and low-key lakeside camping.

Wadis

🌿Wadi ShabTiwi · WadisOne of the most beautiful wadis in the region. Hike through a narrow gorge, swim through turquoise pools and discover a hidden waterfall cave.🏞Wadi ShowkaRas Al Khaimah · WadisA mountain wadi in the Hajar foothills with rock pools, shaded trails and sweeping ridge views — a favourite for hiking, biking and easy camping.

Mountains

🏞Al Rafisah DamSharjah · MountainsAn emerald reservoir ringed by the Hajar mountains near Khorfakkan, with ridge hikes, kayaking and a family-friendly lakeside park on Sharjah's east coast.🌄Jabal YanasSharjah · MountainsA rugged peak above the east coast known for surreal, fiery sunsets and panoramic mountain views — a dramatic spot to camp high above the valleys.⛰Jebel HafeetAl Ain · MountainsThe second highest peak in the UAE. A spectacular winding road climbs 1,240m with panoramic views over Al Ain and the desert beyond.⛰Wadi NaqabRas Al Khaimah · MountainsA dramatic seasonal wadi high in the Hajar Mountains below Jebel Jais — red-rock walls, terraced pools and some of the clearest mountain air and darkest skies in Ras Al Khaimah.

Coast

🏊Dibba RockFujairah · CoastWorld-class snorkelling and diving on the East Coast. Crystal clear water, vibrant coral, and abundant marine life.🪨Shuweihat IslandAbu Dhabi · CoastA remote western island famous for surreal mushroom-shaped rock formations sculpted by wind and sea — raw, sculptural and gloriously empty.🦁Sir Bani Yas IslandAbu Dhabi · CoastA natural island and pioneering wildlife reserve, home to free-roaming Arabian oryx, giraffe and cheetah as part of a decades-long conservation effort.🐠Snoopy IslandFujairah · CoastA rocky island off Al Aqah named for its dog-like profile, ringed by coral and home to turtles, reef sharks and clouds of tropical fish — the UAE's most beloved shore snorkel.
