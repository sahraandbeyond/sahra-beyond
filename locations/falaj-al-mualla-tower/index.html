Falaj Al Mualla Tower

Umm Al Quwain · Dunes · Easy · Best Oct–Apr

Loading live weather…

Rising alone from the open desert, the old watchtower near Falaj Al Mualla is one of Umm Al Quwain's most striking and photogenic landmarks. Falaj Al Mualla is a historic inland oasis, long known for its date plantations and fortifications, and this lone tower — set against nothing but sand and sky — has become a favourite stop for desert drives and photographers.

The surrounding dunes are quiet and accessible, making this an easy, rewarding detour rather than a demanding expedition. It is at its most atmospheric at sunrise and sunset, when the low light turns the sand gold and the tower throws a long shadow across the desert.

Come in the cooler months from October to April, bring water and sun protection, and tread lightly — it is a piece of local heritage in a fragile desert setting. A normal car reaches the area, though the final approach over soft sand can need care.
