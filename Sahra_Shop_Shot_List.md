## Global direction

- **Feel:** premium but human. Real explorers, not models posing. Movement, wind, dust, stillness under stars.
- **Palette:** let each shirt's theme colour lead the frame (warm sand, deep indigo night, dusty rose rock). Avoid competing colours in wardrobe/props.
- **Wardrobe:** neutral bottoms (stone, black, off-white), minimal accessories, bare feet or desert boots. The tee is the hero.
- **Camera:** shoot RAW. Fast lens for blue/▪night hour (f1.4–2.8). Tripod + high ISO discipline for the Milky Way frame.
- **Consistency:** same grade across all three — warm highlights, gentle contrast, filmic shadows. One LUT for the whole drop.
- **Do:** negative space, horizon lines, back-lit rim light, close detail of fabric + print. **Avoid:** midday sun, busy backgrounds, logos/brands, harsh flash.

### Crops we need for the site (shoot wider than you think)

Every hero needs to survive two crops, so **frame with headroom on all sides**:

- **Portrait hero** — tall 4:5 (product section main image + homepage hero). Compose the subject slightly high, room top and bottom.
- **Square card** — 1:1 (homepage featured cards, map popovers). The design/chest must stay inside a centre-weighted square.
- **Wide band** — 16:9 / 21:9 (optional full-width lifestyle band per product section).

## THE SHOTS

### A. Al Quaa Galaxy Tee — night & sky

**A1 · Hero lifestyle (portrait)**
- **Subject:** person wearing the black galaxy tee, standing/seated on desert floor, looking up.
- **Setting:** Al Quaa open desert, clear horizon.
- **Light:** blue hour into night; a soft warm practical (lantern/head-torch glow) on the subject so they read against the dark.
- **Framing:** portrait 4:5, subject lower third, huge sky above. Leave sky room for the square crop too.
- **Styling:** black/stone wardrobe, blanket or thermos as quiet props.
- **Maps to:** `shop-preview.html` Al Quaa section main image → `shirts/lifestyle-alquaa-hero.jpg`; homepage hero option → `shirts/hero-lifestyle.jpg`.

**A2 · Milky Way frame (the money shot)**
- **Subject:** the galaxy arching over a small lone figure in the galaxy tee (or the tee laid/held with sky behind).
- **Setting:** Al Quaa, Bortle 1 sky, no moon (shoot on a new-moon window).
- **Light:** stars only + minimal low warm fill. Tripod, ~15–25s exposure, wide lens.
- **Framing:** portrait for hero; also grab a horizontal for the wide band.
- **Maps to:** galaxy-tee feature / hero background → `shirts/milkyway-galaxy.jpg`.

**A3 · Detail — print + fabric (square/macro)**
- **Subject:** close-up of the chest صحراء mark and the galaxy print across the back; texture of heavyweight organic cotton.
- **Light:** soft directional, raking to show weave and print.
- **Maps to:** product gallery detail slot → `shirts/detail-alquaa.jpg`.

### B. Liwa Dune Tee — golden hour on the dunes

**B1 · Hero lifestyle (portrait)**
- **Subject:** person in the beige Liwa Dune tee walking the ridge of a dune, wind moving the fabric.
- **Setting:** Liwa / Empty Quarter big dunes, clean unspoiled sand.
- **Light:** golden hour, sun low and behind for rim light; long shadows down the dune face.
- **Framing:** portrait 4:5, subject on the diagonal ridge line, sky + sand split.
- **Styling:** tonal — beige tee, stone trousers, barefoot. Nothing that fights the sand palette.
- **Maps to:** `shop-preview.html` Liwa section main → `shirts/lifestyle-liwa-hero.jpg`.

**B2 · Card crop (square)**
- **Subject:** waist-up, back-lit, embroidered sun detail catching the light; dune bokeh behind.
- **Framing:** tight 1:1, chest/print centred.
- **Maps to:** homepage featured card + map popover → `shirts/lifestyle-liwa-card.jpg`.

**B3 · Flat-lay / detail**
- **Subject:** tee laid on rippled sand, a few grains on the fabric, tonal embroidered sun in frame.
- **Light:** low golden side-light for dune-ripple shadows.
- **Maps to:** product gallery detail slot → `shirts/flatlay-liwa.jpg`.
